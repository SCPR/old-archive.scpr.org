Patt Morrison | December 13, 2011

Listen to story

25:32
Download this story 12MB

How many magazines, calendars, swimsuit issues, World War II pinups, television shows and movies have inspired men to wax prophetic about which lovely lady should hold the coveted title of “Sexiest woman ever?"

Men’s Health magazine polled their readers and compiled a list of the top one hundred sexiest women for their most recent issue. Most surprising was that America’s favorite girl next door, Jennifer Aniston took the top spot - over Marilyn, Raquel, Angelina, Cindy, Sophia, Madonna and a host of other women whose legendary physical attributes warrant that they can be recognized by their first name only… as well as many more who may be more recognizable by their sultry eyes, hourglass figures or shapely legs. But is a knockout girl next door sexier than a come hither vixen?

WEIGH IN:

What makes a woman sexy? Who is the sexiest woman of all time? Is she still among us or is classic beauty sexier?

Guest:

Lois Banner, professor of history and gender studies at USC
