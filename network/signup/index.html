What is this all about?
Trustworthy, high-quality journalism starts with knowledgeable sources. We created the Public Insight Network (PIN) to help people like you share what you know with journalists and to learn from the insights and experiences of others.

Who sees my personal information?
We treat your information with utmost care and confidentiality. Only journalists at American Public Media and newsrooms you have chosen to have a relationship with can see your profile information and your insights.

Will I get flooded with spam?
Absolutely not. You're going to get smart, focused emails from working journalists seeking help covering the news. You won't get any unwanted solicitations, nor will we share your contact information with anyone outside of PIN without your explicit permission.

Already in the PIN?
If you're already in the Public Insight Network, creating a profile will allow you to update your information, search for and answer relevant questions on topics important to you, and manage your relationships to journalists in the Network.
