#KidReads Live!

Saturday, July 20, 2013, 10:30am - 12:00pm
A C Bilbrew Public Library 150 E. El Segundo Blvd. Los Angeles, CA 90061 Map and directions

For kids and families, July means summer vacation, and vacation means fun. Throw in a kid-friendly KPCC program about family reading and what do you get? #KidReads Live!

On Saturday, July 20, KPCC's Deepa Fernandes and two literacy whizzes hosted this special offsite event at the A C Bilbrew Library in Los Angeles.  This kid-focused interactive program highlighted ways to make reading fun, effective, and culturally enriching. A mix of discussion and reading with the audience finished with time for audience members to share their favorite book.

Moderator: Deepa Fernandes - KPCC Early Childhood Development Correspondent. @deepaKPCC

Guests:

 

Linda McCorkel Clinard - leading SoCal early literacy expert, retired UC Irvine professor.

 


 



Christabel Nsiah-Buadi - journalist, new mother, founder of Awesome Little Beings storytime sessions. @awesomelilbeing 

 


 

@KPCCforum
#KidReads

This program was made possible in part by L.A. Universal Preschool and its support of our education programming. @LAUP4kids
