Lisa Brenner | November 6, 2012

You can hear it.Every minute, of every hour, onevery frequency, in every fiber, the poundingsound of stomps and brays, the treacherous trumpeting of political provocation, a corrupt ruckus clattering and escalting intoa ringing din of iniquity.Welcome to Election day.Need something fuzzy to look at?

OK.Please enjoy these photographs of cats running governments around the world.

Our domestic dog-and-pony show of donkeys and elephantsis nothing compared to the cat-commanded mouse mongering elsewhere on the planet.Politics is for the clawed, and thismakes the cats very happy.Toensure a future safe for all felines, kittens have fanned out across the globe, positioning themselves close to the ballots, and, whenever possible, close the world leaders themselves.

Now go vote. The cats are watching.
