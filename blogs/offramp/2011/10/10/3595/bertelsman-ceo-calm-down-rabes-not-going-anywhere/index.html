John Rabe | October 10, 2011

This headline in Variety ...

Rabe to head Bertelsmann

... got my friends got all excited. Some thought I deserved it. Reading further, it sure sounds like me.

Gunter Thielen, former Bertelsmann CEO and current chairman of the company's supervisory board, said of Rabe, "We are sure that in him, we have an entrepreneur at the top who will lend further momentum to Bertelsmann's growth strategy and future development."

Well, I do deserve to head the German media giant, and I am an "entrepreneur at the top," or an "Unternehmer an der Spitze," but one look at the photo should tell you you're laboring under the misapprehension that there could only be one person with such a silly last name.

This is new Bertelsmann chief Thomas Rabe:

And this is me, John Rabe:

Obviously, not the same person. I don't even know Hartmut Ostrowski.

For the record, I'm also not:

Famous playwrite David Rabe ...

U-35 crewmember Dr Karl Rabe...

The Good Man of Nanking, John Rabe ... or this handsome man:

University of Minnesota tight end, John Rabe.

I'm the host of Off-Ramp, a weekly news and arts show Variety probably doesn't even know exists.
